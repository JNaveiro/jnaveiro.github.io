Notes

    Some useful formulae for homogeneous spaces
    In these notes I derive (mainly following Kobayashi-Nomizu) several formulas that I have found very useful for working with reductive homogeneous spaces.

Reading group

From Autumn 2021 until Spring 2023 we held a reading group on the book Lectures on Real Semisimple Lie Algebras and their Representations, by A. L. Onishchik.
In this website you can find recordings and handwritten notes for Chapters 1 to 6.

Miscellaneous course materials

These are some spare materials that I have written for teaching purposes. You will most probably not find them interesting.

  • Sistemas de EDO lineales (in Spanish, aimed at first year Biotechnology students).
    In this document we explain how to take the exponential of an arbitrary \(2\times 2\) real matrix in order to solve linear systems of differential equations.
  • Transporte paralelo na esfera (in Galician, aimed at first year Mathematics students).
    Here I provide an elementary way of deriving the equations of parallel translation along a parallel of the unit sphere \(\mathsf{S}^{2}\).
