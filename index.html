About me

I am a third year PhD student at the Department of Mathematics of Universidade de Santiago de Compostela (Spain), and a PhD student member of the Galician Center for Mathematical Research and Technology (CITMAGA).
My PhD thesis is supervised by Prof. José Carlos Díaz-Ramos and revolves around the topic of submanifold geometry in Riemannian manifolds with symmetry (mainly homogeneus and symmetric spaces).
